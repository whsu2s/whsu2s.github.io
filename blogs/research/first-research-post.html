Posted on April 28, 2025

This scientific article investigates a novel approach to understanding multiple sclerosis (MS) using a deep learning model called StyleGAN. The research trained the StyleGAN model on MRI scans of both MS patients and healthy individuals. By manipulating images in the model's latent space, the study successfully simulated MS progression, observing changes like brain volume loss and increased lesion size. This demonstrates the model's potential for identifying imaging markers and gaining further insight into the relationship between brain atrophy and the advancement of MS.
